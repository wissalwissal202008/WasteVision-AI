# WasteVision AI – No Voice Features

**This app does not use voice recognition, speech-to-text, text-to-speech, or audio prompts.**

- Do not add microphone input, spoken commands, or “ask aloud” features.
- Do not add TTS (e.g. expo-speech) to read results or coach answers aloud.
- Do not add STT or voice correction flows.

All interaction is text and tap only. Ignore any past or suggested voice features in other docs.
