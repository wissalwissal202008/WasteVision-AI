# WasteVision AI – 30-Second Pitch

**Use this for demos, startup contests, or quick introductions.**

---

## Version 1 (Problem → Solution → Impact)

> **The problem:** Most people don’t know how to sort waste. Wrong bins mean recyclable materials end up in landfills.
>
> **Our solution:** WasteVision AI turns your phone into a smart recycling assistant. You take a photo of any object — bottle, can, packaging — and the app tells you what it is, what it’s made of, and which bin to use. If the AI is wrong, you correct it, and the system learns. We combine computer vision with human feedback to improve accuracy over time.
>
> **Impact:** We help citizens, schools, and municipalities sort better, reduce contamination, and make sustainability simple. WasteVision AI shows how AI can tackle real environmental challenges every day.

---

## Version 2 (One-liner + Hook)

> **One-liner:** WasteVision AI is a smart recycling assistant: point your phone at any waste, get instant sorting advice, and help the AI learn from your corrections.
>
> **Hook:** Every correction you make doesn’t just fix one result — it makes the app smarter for everyone. We’re turning everyday users into the training data for better waste management.

---

## Version 3 (Elevator – 2 sentences)

> WasteVision AI uses AI and your smartphone camera to tell you exactly where to throw any object — plastic, glass, metal, paper — with clear recycling and eco tips. When you correct a mistake, the app gets smarter, so we improve recycling accuracy and environmental impact together.

---

## Key numbers to mention (when you have them)

- **6 categories** of waste (plastic, paper/carton, glass, metal, organic, non-recyclable)
- **One tap** from photo to result
- **Human-in-the-loop**: corrections used to retrain the model
- **Multi-platform**: iOS, Android, Web (Expo + React Native)
